color: determines the text color
font-family - Family of fonts
font-size: Size of the font (normally set at the body level and then we use rem)
font-style: normal/italic/oblique
font-weight: normal/bold etc
text-transform: none, uppercase, lowercase, capitalize
text-decoration: none, underline, overline

text-align: left right justify
line-height: 1.5- 2
letter-spacing: spaces between letters
word-spacing: spaces between words
